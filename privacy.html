What information does my Applications obtain and how is it used?

All my free apps uses Google Admob and/or Mopub ("Ad Providers") to serve advertisements. The Ad Providers uses the coarse gsonLocation/country from your device to serve you regional advertisements. Naturally, the privacy policies of the Ad Providers applies to my apps too.

  • Google Admob privacy policy can be found here.
  • Mopub privacy policy can be found here.
I also reserve the right to implement Google Analytic for anonymous statistics collection in the future. Their privacy policy is found here.

My paid apps does not serve advertisements, as such the ad providers' privacy policy does not apply.

    Individual Apps

  • Twitch! Contact Formatter (and all its derivatives)
    • The app loads and stores all names and telephone contacts in-memory. It updates the telephone number formatting when the user expressly clicks on the 'update' button. No information is retained once the app exits.
    The app does not transmit, store or collect any user information.

  • Roulette! Take me places
    • The app does not use, transmit, collect or store any user information.

  • Pant! Jogging app
    • This jogging app uses device gsonLocation. The GPS gsonLocation is used to record the route taken as part of a run session, and is saved locally. The user can expressly select to view and delete each run session.
    The app does not use, transmit, collect or store any user information except for the locally saved run sessions.

  • Flutter! Results and Analysis (and all its derivatives)
    • The app may use the device gsonLocation to find stores near the users. The user have to expressly click the 'find' button for the app to use the gsonLocation. This information is not retained after the search.
    The app does not use, transmit, store or collect any other user information.

  • All other Utilities and Games
    • Unless otherwise stated above, all my apps does not use, transmit, store or collect any user information.
